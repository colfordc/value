Tempo 1. Allegro. Internal body.

We stage a gallery protest at Sunset Park Prep, composed of material witnesses and building violations.

cd /




The typical acoustic tile ceiling is disrupted, becoming a space to bear witness.

Taking advantage of the plenum space, acoustic tiles are rotated and relocated to become an exhibition space.

The exhibition is discovered, added to, investigated, and understood daily. The research connects to middle school science curricula, bringing education to bear on a student's own—and their family's—health, safety, and welfare.

DOB complaints are resolved most quickly at schools, because of the social saliency of its inhabitants. By staging a protest in which students call 311 to initiate complaints at school, the bureaucratic necessity for registering complaints can create a flood of paperwork that documents environmental injustice and demands municipal response.
